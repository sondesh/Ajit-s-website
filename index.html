by Asia's 50 Best Restaurants 2019 for the fifth consecutive year.
It has also been rated as the No.1 Restaurant in India by
TripAdvisor consistently from 2014 - 2019. Indian Accent has been awarded
the Number 1 Restaurant in India by Condé Nast Traveller and
Himalayan Sparkling Top Restaurant Awards.
The romance of the deccan of India comes alive at this authentic,
award winning restaurant, making it a landmark dining destination in PUNE.
